As a part of a larger project named SMART Ala Wai for the Strategic Monitoring and Resiliance Training in the Alawai Watershed, these loggers record pressure, temperature, and RGB light levels (some data below). My role in this particular iteration included chassis design, assembly, and software testing to ensure the loggers were running smoothly before distribution. 30 of the loggers that we made were given to middle and high school teachers attending a workshop we hosted in April, for the crowd-sourced effort to gain a better understanding of our watershed.
