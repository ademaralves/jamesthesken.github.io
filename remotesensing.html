Remote Sensing

Summer 2017 I had the opportunity to work on my beautiful home of Kaua'i. Spent the summer funded by the Hawai'i Space Grant Consortium to help Kaua'i Coffee draft a plan for data collection, interpretation, and implementation into their year-to-year operations.

Computer Vision

Using OpenCV and Python, it was interesting to explore potential use cases for a drone on a coffee farm. Aside from the pretty pictures you can take with a drone, a few ideas that are in the works include tree counting, yield estimation, and collaborative UAS. Perhaps with some further encouragement I will post more info on these.

Data Management and Interpretation 

The biggest goal I have in this space is to quantitatively analyze data we receive from each flight. I use off-the-shelf programs such as PrecisionMapper and Atlas to comminicate crop issues and areas of interest to field managers.
