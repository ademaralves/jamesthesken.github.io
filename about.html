About Me

Born on the island of O'ahu, raised on the south side of Kaua'i. I was extremely fortunate to have lived within a 5 minute walk from the beach. Every day after school since the fourth grade involved ~2 hours in the water, or of course ~5+ hours surfing on the weekends.

One of the best things I love about engineering is that time put in almost always equals the results out. Just like surfing.
