About Me

Born on the island of O'ahu, raised on the south side of Kaua'i. I was extremely fortunate to have lived within a 5 minute walk from the beach. Every day after school since the fourth grade involved ~2 hours in the water, or of course ~5+ hours surfing on the weekends.

One of the best things I love about engineering is that time put in, always equals the results out. Just like surfing.

But, why engineering?

I think about this question a lot. Life would be a lot easier had I decided to run away to the Mentawais and surf forever.

It was probably in the seventh grade when I caught the science fiction bug. I had watched the 1968 classic Planet of the Apes with Charlton Heston. After that I found every Arthur C. Clarke book I could at the local library.

Obligitory top 5 Sci-fi novels:

  • 2001: A Space Odyssey (really the whole series)
  • Childhood's End
  • The Oil Eater
  • Dune
  • Rendezvous with Rama
